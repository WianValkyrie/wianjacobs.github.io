Hyades Rain

Hyades Rain is a story-driven RPG project that I contributed to as the sole programmer and Unity developer. Inspired by games like Disco Elysium and XCOM, this ambitious project was undertaken with a small team in 2021.

Unfortunately, the project was put on hold and never resumed, but it remains a valuable experience in my portfolio.
