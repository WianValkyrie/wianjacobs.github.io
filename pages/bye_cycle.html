Bye-Cycle

Bye-Cycle is a cheeky 2D mobile game I created with Unity, where players are encouraged to drive with reckless abandon and cause as much chaos as possible. Players can use their phone's gyroscope to control their vehicle and complete challenges that involve disrupting road works, triggering car accidents, and even knock cyclists off their bikes.
