From Scan to Scene

This gallery presents a complete, start-to-finish showcase of my 3D environment creation workflow. I handled every aspect of the process, from initial 3D scanning, to data cleaning and post-processing, followed by mesh creation and 3D modeling, and finally, detailed texturing and lighting, ultimately producing a realistic, engine-ready environment that's adaptable to various applications.
